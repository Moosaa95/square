ABOUT Srum & Associates Ltd

Our Story

Srum & Associates Ltd is a renowned public limited liability company incorporated on May 19th, 2015, under the Nigerian law. We are a distinguished construction firm specializing in modern engineering construction, road network and drainages, project supervision, consultancy, cost management and control. With a keen eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as a leader in the industry. We also engage in counterpart project execution with reputable construction companies known for their core professionalism, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible services.
